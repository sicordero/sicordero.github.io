Field team member for ROSETTA-Ice Project - an interdisciplinary, airborne, geophysical survey of the Ross Ice Shelf in Antarctica. Institutional collaborators: Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory at Columbia University, Scripps Institute of Oceanography, Colorado College, U.S. Geological Survey, and Earth & Space Research.
    * Scientific Support: Airborne ice-penetrating radar QC specialist for ROSETTA for Antarctic field deployments (2016-2017, 2017-2018).
    * Project Management (Ramp-Up): Field instrument and cargo organization, packing, and international shipping to-and-from Antarctica. Reviewed and streamlined field instrument operating procedures, QC procedures, QC report forms, data archive procedures, and additional documentation forms
    * Participated in RAC tent set-up for on-site digital data processing and archiving, including: cable management, field report organization, and engineering bay tool organization
    * Geophysical Sensors QC'd: Deep ICE (DICE) radar, Shallow Ice Radar (SIR), Airborne Magnetometer, GPS systems (POD-INS/SPAN)
